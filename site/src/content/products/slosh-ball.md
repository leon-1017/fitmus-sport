<div class="fusion-title title fusion-title-size-two" style="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:31px;"><h2 class="title-heading-left">Feature</h2><div class="title-sep-container"><div class="title-sep sep-double sep-solid" style="border-color:#e03420;"></div></div></div><div class="fusion-text"><p>Slosh Balls take training with water filled implements to new heights of excitement and difficulty.Slosh balls are an amazing stability training tool. These water filled reinforced plastic balls mimic atlas stones and allow you to perform strongman lifts such as stone lifts, bear hugs and shouldering drills… without the high cost and risk of injury! The water makes them move erratically. They’ll challenge every muscle in your body while testing your concentration. Lose focus and the Slosh Ball becomes an untamed monster. You’re forced to learn to lift correctly or you won’t be able to hold onto it.<br>
And don’t worry about dropping it. It won’t break and the cushion of air easily absorbs the impact so you won’t damage the floor.<br>
Slosh Balls are safe and effective way of training every muscle in your body<br>
Pump included and&nbsp;Water not included.</p>
