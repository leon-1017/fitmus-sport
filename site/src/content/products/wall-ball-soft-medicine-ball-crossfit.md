## Feature

Wall Ball Soft Medicine Ball – Crossfit uses a 1.2 mm synthetic-leather cover with rubber-particle filler for balanced handling and colour coding. The soft, layered construction is designed for cardio, CrossFit and strength-building exercises including tosses, catches, squat throws, twists and side throws.

Its impact-absorbing design distributes weight evenly to help maintain shape during normal wall-ball use. It is intended for wall-ball activity and should not be slammed on hard surfaces.

## Brief Description

- Diameter: approximately 35 cm (14 in)
- Colours: purple, orange, red, black, grey, yellow and green
- Available weights: 6 lb, 8 lb, 10 lb, 12 lb, 14 lb, 16 lb, 18 lb, 20 lb, 25 lb and 30 lb
