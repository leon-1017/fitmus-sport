## Feature

Water Rowing Machine simulates the action of watercraft rowing for indoor exercise and training. Its smooth, self-regulated water resistance supports calorie burn while engaging major muscle groups, with core-strengthening benefits and the low impact associated with indoor rowing.

The pure wooden domestic water-resistance rower uses wood imported from Europe and the United States, integrating with home furnishings for high-end home fitness.
