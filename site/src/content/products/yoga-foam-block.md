## Feature

Yoga Foam Block is suitable for amateur and professional yoga practice. It supports improved form, alignment and flexibility, and its durable, supportive EVA foam provides stability and balance for deeper poses and increased strength.

The block is large enough to sit on comfortably. Spot clean with mild detergent, then towel or air dry.

## Brief Description

- Product size: 9 in W × 6 in H × 4 in D
- Material: durable EVA foam
- Density options: 120 g/cm³ or 200 g/cm³
- Available in a variety of styles, colours and shapes
