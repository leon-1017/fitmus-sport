## Feature

Fitmus Wall Balls are individually constructed, stitched and tested for quality for CrossFit exercises. Reinforced panels and advanced stitching support durability and shape retention, while specialised panels provide grip.

The ball uses 18 pieces of 1.2 mm synthetic leather and is designed for balanced handling. Available colours include purple, orange, red, black, grey, yellow and green.

## Brief Description

Uniform diameter: approximately 35 cm.

Available weights: 6 lb, 8 lb, 10 lb, 14 lb, 16 lb and 20 lb.
