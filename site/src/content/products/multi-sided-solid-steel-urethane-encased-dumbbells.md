<p>Solid steel urethane dumbbells are the most durable and maintenance free dumbbell choice on the market. Each Iron Grip dumbbell utilizes your choice of 30mm solid steel round handle or contoured handles that are press fit through each 12-sided solid steel head and then fully welded for an inseparable fusion. This one piece construction utilizes no bolts, screws, glue, pins or anything else that has the potential to fail in extreme weightlifting environments. After each welded solid steel dumbbell is complete the 12-sided anti-roll heads are then encapsulated in a protective polyurethane. This urethane coating is paramount in retaining the dumbbells esthetics year after year and will never crack, peel or chip like other dumbbells can. Urethane is also superior in helping protect floors and other adjacent strength equipment from impact caused by dropping dumbbells.</p>
