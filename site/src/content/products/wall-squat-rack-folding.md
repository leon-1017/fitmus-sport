## Feature

Folding Wall Squat Rack includes a pair of J-cups, four wall-mounting anchors and four interlocking rods. Plastic covers at the base of the vertical posts help protect the floor surface and improve stability.

When folded, the rack uses approximately 5 in of space. It is compatible with functional-cage accessories and uses a traction bar and inward-folding sides for quick closing against the wall.

## Brief Description

- Folding wall squat rack model
- Approximate wall width: 120 cm
- Approximate depth: 60 cm
- Height: 230 cm
- Tube size: 75 × 75 × 3 mm
- Approximate weight: 70 kg
- Safety-pin buckle for fast folding and opening
- Rubber pads on the two front columns to protect the floor
