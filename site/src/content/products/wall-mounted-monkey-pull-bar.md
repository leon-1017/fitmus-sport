## Feature

Wall Mounted Multi-Grip Pull-Up Bar can be used as a stand-alone section or joined into a continuous multi-bracketed system. It is suitable for CrossFit boxes, martial-arts training, gyms and home setups. Custom powder coating on brackets is available.

## Brief Description

- Textured matte-black powder coat
- Steel tube specification: 50 × 50 × 2 mm and 32 × 32 × 2 mm
- Net weight: 23 kg
- Stated load capacity: 500 lb
