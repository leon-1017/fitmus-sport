Each Sandbag has a built-in filler bag with an additional zipper and hook-and-loop closure—ensuring filler material stays fully contained as you work through your regimen.<br>
Strongman Sandbags should not be loaded over the prescribed weight. Use scales to ensure you have not overloaded the bags.Use sand only. Do not use pebbles, gravel or any other aggregate material.<br>
Filler material is not provided with Strongman Sandbags. Purchase sand from your local builders merchant.<br>
Bags are designed to endure being dropped and not to be slammed.</p>
