## Feature

Wall mounted Rig is suitable for gyms, commercial facilities and home use. It can be used with resistance bands, TRX systems, Olympic bars, push-up accessories, benches and other fitness equipment.

This wall-mounted format is designed as a space-saving option for gyms and CrossFit boxes. Length, attachments and colour can be tailored to the installation. The rig combines squat stands, chin-up capability and compatible attachments in a strong, economical wall-based configuration.

The rig uses 75 × 75 × 3 mm steel with cut holes on all four sides for mounting and accessory placement.

## Brief Description

This 3-3 wall-mounted rig concept includes:

- 6 × 2510 mm uprights (75 × 75 × 3 mm, four-side cut holes with cut numbers)
- 8 × 1570 mm chin-up bars
- 3 × 1070 mm chin-up bars
- All required nuts and bolts

Approximate dimensions: W6800 × D1645 × H2510 mm.
