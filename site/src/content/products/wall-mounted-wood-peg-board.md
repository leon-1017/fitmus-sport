<div class="fusion-title title fusion-title-size-two" style="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:31px;"><h2 class="title-heading-left">Feature</h2><div class="title-sep-container"><div class="title-sep sep-double sep-solid" style="border-color:#e03420;"></div></div></div><div class="fusion-text"><p>Wall-mounted FITMUS Wood Peg Board is made in the CHINA from your choice of top-quality thickness birch.Each set includes one 1.5″(3.8cm-4.0cm) thick board 95.625”(2430mm) tall x 11.75”wide(300mm) &nbsp;and two grip-friendly wood dowel rods—each measuring&nbsp; 6.5”(16cm) in length and 1.19”(30mm) in diameter.</p>
<p>An increasingly popular tool for upper body training, climbing peg boards can be a simple but dynamic addition to a small garage gym or a larger training facility.</p>
<p>This design includes 35 staggered holes along the roughly 8-foot ascent of the wood board, with six counter bored holes for mounting (mounting hardware not included). The full unit weighs in at 38 pounds.</p>
<p>Your branding burned into wood, running up the face of the board or<br>
Black Washer Plates installed at both ends of the board with laser-cut Your branding.</p>
<p>Please Note: Peg boards can be mounted vertically or horizontally (mounting holes are spaced 40” apart and 9.25” across). Whatever setup you choose, be sure to mount to a secure wall that can handle the weight of the board itself PLUS any static and dynamic loads being applied to the unit.</p>
