## Feature

The FITMUS wall-mounted wood peg board is an upper-body training and climbing tool for garage gyms and larger training facilities. Each set includes a 1.5 in (3.8–4.0 cm) thick wood board and two grip-friendly wood dowel rods.

The board has 35 staggered holes along its roughly eight-foot ascent and six counter-bored mounting holes. It can be mounted vertically or horizontally; install it on a secure wall capable of carrying both the board and applied static and dynamic loads.

## Brief Description

- Wood board available in pine or maple
- Approximate board size: 95.625 in (243 cm) tall × 11.75 in (29.8 cm) wide × 1.5 in (3.8 cm) thick
- 35 staggered holes
- 2 unfinished hickory pegs / dowel rods: 6.5 in (16.5 cm) long × 1.19 in (3 cm) diameter
- 6 counter-bored mounting holes
- Mounting holes spaced approximately 40 in apart and 9.25 in across
