## Feature

Wooden Balance Board is designed to integrate balance work into fitness, sport performance and rehabilitation. It can help rebuild strength after sporting injuries and sprains and support rehabilitation for feet, ankle and knee injuries. It also trains the abdomen and back muscles.

The board uses a non-slip surface with textured round-ball elements.

## Brief Description

- Material: wood
- Colour: black with yellow
- Incline: 24 degrees
- Diameter: 16 in (40 cm)
