## Feature

X-Frame 6 Rack Package is suitable for CrossFit and personal-training groups. It includes six squat racks with multiple chin-up and rings stations. The single chin-up cross beams can be inverted to vary chin-up height.

It is built from 65 mm square steel tube with a 4 mm wall thickness, laser-cut for a professional fit and finished with a durable black powder coat.

## Brief Description

- 12 × 2.59 m standard upright bars
- 6 × 43 in triangle Dirty South bars
- 4 × 70 in triangle Dirty South bars
- 6 × 70 in single cross beams
- 12 × J-hooks (pieces)
- Approximate dimensions: 7382 × 2043 × 2600 mm
- Made for commercial use
- Colour options: red, black, blue, green or orange
