## Feature

Wall Crossfit Rack is a commercial-grade, wall-mounted strength-training station designed for functional training, weightlifting and CrossFit-style use.

## Brief Description

- Overall size: 1190 × 1800 × 2510 mm
- 2 × 2.5 m upright bars (65 × 65 × 4 mm, two-side cut holes)
- 2 × 1.8 m double crossbeams (32 × 32 × 2.5 mm)
- 4 × 1.1 m single crossbeams
- 1 pair of J-hooks
- 1 pair of spotter arms
