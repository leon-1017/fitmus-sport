## Feature

Fitmus Wooden Puzzle Plyo Box is made from 18 mm plywood. Its form-fitting levels can be configured for 8 in, 12 in and 20 in heights.

The three-level box uses a CNC-machined puzzle design for a precise finish and space-saving trapezoidal form. It ships flat with hardware and pre-drilled holes for fast, glueless assembly.

## Brief Description

Available height configuration: 8 in, 12 in and 20 in.
