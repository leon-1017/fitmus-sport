## Feature

Wall Mounted Rig 2-2 is suitable for gym, commercial and home use. It can be used with resistance bands, TRX systems, Olympic bars, push-up accessories and benches. The space-saving rig can be configured with personalised length, attachments and colour.

The rig uses 75 × 75 × 3 mm steel uprights with cut holes on all four sides.

## Brief Description

- 4 × 2510 mm uprights (75 × 75 × 3 mm, four-side cut holes with number markings)
- 5 × 1570 mm chin-up bars
- 2 × 1070 mm chin-up bars
- All nuts and bolts included
- Approximate dimensions: W4010 × D1645 × H2510 mm
