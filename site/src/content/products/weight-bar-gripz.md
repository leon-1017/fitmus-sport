## Feature

Weight Bar Gripz fit barbells, dumbbells, chin-up bars and cable attachments. They are designed for standard and Olympic bars with an approximate 25–28 mm diameter.

Made from high-density silicone, the grips clamp securely around the bar. Available in blue, green and orange; sold as a pair.
