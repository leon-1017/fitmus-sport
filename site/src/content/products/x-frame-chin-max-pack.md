## Feature

X-Frame Chin Up Max Pack is suitable for CrossFit and personal-training groups. It includes four squat racks, multiple chin-up and rings stations, plus a monkey-bar feature for varied cross-training drills.

The single chin-up cross beams can be inverted to vary height, while monkey-bar cross beams can be removed or adjusted to change their distance. The structure uses 65 mm square steel tube with a 4 mm wall thickness, laser-cut for a professional fit and finished with durable black powder coat.

## Brief Description

- 10 × 2.59 m standard upright bars
- 5 × 70 in single cross beams
- 4 × 43 in multifunctional cross beams
- 6 × 70 in monkey bars
- 4 × pairs of X-Frame J-hooks
- 40 × high-tensile nuts and bolts
- Assembled size: 6069 × 1910 × 2600 mm high
- Made for commercial use
