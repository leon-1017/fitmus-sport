## Feature

Wall Mount Rig supports core training, pull-ups, squats, bench press, dips and suspension training. It is designed as an all-in-one wall-mounted rig for studios, personal training, CrossFit boxes and fitness centres. It can also be used as a free-standing rig when wall bars are not incorporated.

## Brief Description

- Capacity: 340 kg (750 lb)
- Intended use: company, home or small gym
- 2 × 2.61 m, 4 mm thick, powder-coated steel uprights
- 1 × 1.07 m triangular pull-up bar
- 2 × 1.12 m single wall bars
- 2 × nylon-coated J-cups
- 1 × pin-lock dip bar
- 1 × landmine sleeve
