## Feature

The 30 kg Weight Vest is a training aid for explosive and sport-specific movement. It can be used for weighted bodyweight training, calisthenics, push-ups, dips, pull-ups, gymnastic-ring training, burpees, running, sprints and plyometrics.

The vest accepts 0.5 kg and 1 kg weight plates up to 30 kg. Its double-strap system is designed to keep the vest secure during training.

## Brief Description

- Total weight options: 15 kg or 30 kg
- Individual weight blocks: 0.5 kg and 1 kg
- One size fits all
- Colour: black
- Front-and-back weight distribution
- Heavy-duty reinforced nylon and adjustable hook-and-loop straps
