<p>The Secret behind the Rip:60 Suspension system</p>

The rip:60’s straps are designed to rotate back and forth creating a greater connection between your mind and muscle. This innovative suspension turns on the nervous system and trains the muscles in your body to work together. This ultimately results in greater balance, stability and coordination.</p>
<p>Optimization Zone<br>
Whether you are overweight or trying to build lean muscle, the rip:60 suspension system works and heres WHY: When you begin to fatigue, you can step forward or backward, without losing form, to decrease or increase the load.
