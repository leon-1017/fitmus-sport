## Feature

Wall Mount Single Rig is a single-frame setup suitable for up to three people at once, with one squat rack and two chin-up stations. Its single chin-up crossbeams can be inverted to vary the chin-up height. The rig uses laser-cut 65 mm square steel tube with a durable black powder-coat finish.

## Brief Description

- 2 × 2.5 m standard uprights
- 2 × 43 in single crossbeams / chin-up bars
- 1 × 43 in multi-grip pull-up crossbeam
- 1 pair of X-Frame J-hooks
- 8 × high-tensile nuts and bolts
- Approximate assembled size: 1222 mm wide × 1157 mm deep × 2600 mm high
