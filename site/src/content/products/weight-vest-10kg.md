## Feature

The 10 kg Weight Vest adds resistance as strength improves and can be used for walking, jogging, rope jumping, cycling, core strengthening and weight training.

Its double-strap system is designed to fasten the vest securely during a workout. The vest uses adjustable weight blocks and front-and-back weight distribution.

## Brief Description

- Total weight option: 10 kg or 5 kg
- Individual weight blocks: 0.5 kg and 1 kg
- One size fits all
- Colour: black
- Heavy-duty reinforced nylon construction
- Adjustable hook-and-loop straps
