## Feature

Yoga Wheel Back Stretcher Wheel is a handcrafted back roller designed to open the back and shoulders, align the spine, relax muscles, expand the chest, and stretch hip flexors and quadriceps. It supports chest, shoulder, back, hip and psoas opening; muscle-tension release; core and back strength; balance; and deeper back bends.

## Brief Description

- Material: 6 mm TPE foam with PC or ABS inner tube
- Colour options: pink, purple, blue or other customised colours
- Size: 32 cm diameter × 13 cm wheel height
- Custom logo available
- Applications: yoga, gymnastics and fitness
- Load test: 300 kg
