## Feature

Wall Mounted Rig 1-1 is a space-saving option for gyms, CrossFit boxes and home installations. It can be used with resistance bands, TRX systems, Olympic bars, benches and other functional-training equipment. Length, attachments and colour can be customised.

The rig uses 75 × 75 × 3 mm steel with cut holes on all four sides for mounting and accessory placement.

## Brief Description

- 2 × 2510 mm uprights (75 × 75 × 3 mm, four-side cut holes with number markings)
- 2 × 1570 mm chin-up bars
- 1 × 1070 mm chin-up bar
- All nuts and bolts included
- Approximate dimensions: W1220 × D1645 × H2510 mm
