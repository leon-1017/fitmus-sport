## Feature

VIPR Storage Rack is designed to neatly store ViPR loaded movement tools. Its dedicated structure supports easy loading and unloading while keeping equipment organised, and can hold up to 14 ViPRs.

FITMUS also supplies rig storage accessories, racks and attachments for convenient storage of Olympic weight plates, kettlebells, medicine and exercise balls, power bags and ViPR tools.

## Brief Description

- Storage capacity: up to 14 ViPRs
- Approximate dimensions: 39 in L × 30 in W × 57 in H
