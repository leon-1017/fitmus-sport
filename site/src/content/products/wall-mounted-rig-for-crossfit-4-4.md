## Feature

Wall mounted Rig for Crossfit 4-4 is suitable for gym, commercial and home use. It can be used with resistance bands, TRX systems, Olympic bars, push-up accessories and benches. The space-saving rig can be configured with personalised length, attachments and colour.

The rig combines an aggressive wing and squat stands with attachment compatibility. It uses 75 × 75 × 3 mm steel uprights with cut holes on all four sides for wall installation.

## Brief Description

- 8 × 2510 mm uprights (75 × 75 × 3 mm, four-side cut holes with number markings)
- 11 × 1570 mm chin-up bars
- 4 × 1070 mm chin-up bars
- All nuts and bolts included
- Approximate dimensions: W9590 × D1645 × H2510 mm
