## Feature

Wall Mount Rig is available in four standard lengths: 10’, 14’, 20’ and 24’. It can be installed by bolting the unit to the floor and a solid wall, including concrete, cinder block or wood.

The system is fully expandable in 4’ and 6’ sections and is compatible with a wide range of accessories.

## Brief Description

Commercial-grade Wall Mount Rig made from 75 × 75 × 3 mm steel. Standard lengths include 10 ft, 14 ft, 20 ft and 24 ft.

### 14 ft configuration

- 4 × 2750 mm uprights (75 × 75 × 3 mm)
- 5 × 1800 mm double crossbeams
- 2 × 1100 mm double crossbeams
- 2 pairs of J-cup hooks

Approximate assembled dimensions: 2750 mm high × 4267 mm wide × 1950 mm deep. Finished in black. Bench, bar and plates are not included.
