## Feature

Wood Jerk Box Set blocks are stackable and interlocking to help keep the setup stable during heavy training. They use 20 mm plywood, internal bracing and polished sides and corners. Top stoppers help prevent a barbell from rolling off the stack.

## Brief Description

- 8 blocks per set: 2 × 2.25 in, 2 × 6 in, 2 × 12 in and 2 × 15 in
- Block footprint: 91 × 51 cm
- Adjustable height: approximately 6–89 cm
- Complete-set weight: 180 kg
- Maximum stated load: 500 kg
- Fully assembled; the 2.25 in block must be on top when in use
