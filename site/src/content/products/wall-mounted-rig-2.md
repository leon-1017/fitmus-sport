## Feature

Wall Mounted Rig provides a compact solution for home gyms, company gyms and CrossFit facilities. It supports core training, pull-ups, squats, bench press, dips and suspension training, and can also be used as a free-standing rig when wall bars are not incorporated.

## Brief Description

- Overall size: 1100 × 1100 × 2160 mm
- 2 × 2.61 m uprights (65 × 65 × 4 mm)
- 2 × 1.1 m double crossbeams
- 1 × 1.1 m single crossbeam
- 4 × 1.1 m monkey-bar rods
- Pair of J-cups, safety pins and spotter arms
- Stated capacity: 340 kg (750 lb)
