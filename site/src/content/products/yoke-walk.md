## Feature

Yoke Walk is a durable, cost-effective training solution for versatile strength and conditioning programmes. It supports squatting, pressing, benching, dragging, pushing and yoke carries, and is suitable for commercial, home and garage training setups.

## Brief Description

- Elite yoke height: 1.8 m (72 in), including base
- Footprint: 1.3 × 1.3 m
- 3 mm steel uprights with 27 mm hole spacing for J-peg placement
- 60 mm diameter tube for the yoke crossmember
- 48 mm thick storage tube for bumper plates
- Approximate yoke weight: 89 kg
- Weight capacity: 350 kg
- Includes two uprights, two bases, four storage feet, two side-storage tubes, two J-hooks and one adjustable-height crossbeam
