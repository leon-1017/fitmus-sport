## Feature

Fitmus wood gymnastic rings are CNC machined and hand sanded from 1.11 in Baltic birch plywood for FIG-style use or 1.25 in birch for gym use. Numbered 18 ft cam-buckle straps make it easier to set both rings to the same height and adjust them quickly.

## Included

- 2 × 1.5 in wide, 18 ft numbered straps
- 2 × Baltic birch wood rings
- 2 × cam buckles
- Velcro straps to secure excess webbing

## Brief Description

- Ring diameter options: 1.11 in or 1.25 in
- Outside diameter: 9.25 in
- Inside diameter: 6.75 in
- Strap length and width: 18 ft × 1.5 in
- Pair weight: 1.8 kg
- Rated capacity: 600 lb
