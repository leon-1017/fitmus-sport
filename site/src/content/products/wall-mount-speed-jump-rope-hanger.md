## Feature

Steel Wall Mounted Jump Rope Hanger is an organiser rack for growing training facilities. Finished in black powder coat, it has 15 storage slots for rope handles; cords can also be hung over the rungs for additional capacity. It mounts to a secure gym surface with horizontally and vertically spaced mounting holes.

## Brief Description

- 15 storage slots, designed for 4 handles / 2 ropes per slot
- Storage capacity: up to 30 ropes
- Approximate dimensions: 23.5 in L × 4 in H (60 × 10.2 cm)
- Depth: 5.125 in (13 cm)
- Steel construction with black powder-coat finish
- Mounting hardware included
