## Feature

Weight Lifting Chains slide over the ends of a bar. As the lift rises, more chain leaves the floor and resistance progressively increases through the range of motion. The zinc-plated finish helps resist rust.

Fitmus can assemble fitness, exercise, CrossFit and weight-lifting chain configurations to specification using suitable materials and methods.

## Brief Description

Available weights: 5 kg, 8 kg, 10 kg, 15 kg, 20 kg, 25 kg and 30 kg.
