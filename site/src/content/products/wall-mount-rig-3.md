## Feature

This Wall Mounted Rig combines squat stands, attachment compatibility and a space-efficient footprint for a home gym or affiliate. Height, pull-up bars, accessories and colour can be customised; source colour options include red, black, green, orange, yellow and blue.

## Brief Description

- 2 × 3.5 m uprights (65 mm, 4 mm thick, two-sided holes)
- 2 × 2.5 m uprights (65 mm, 4 mm thick, two-sided holes)
- 1 × 1.8 m single crossbeam
- 6 × 1.1 m single crossbeams
- All nuts and bolts included
- Approximate dimensions: W4000 × D1100 × H3500 mm
