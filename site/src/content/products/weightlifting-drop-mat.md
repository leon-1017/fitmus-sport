## Feature

Weightlifting Drop Pads are designed to reduce noise and vibration from barbell drops. Each pad measures 100 × 60 × 15 cm, providing a controlled landing area while remaining manageable to move and store.

The pair uses a non-skid base, reinforced handles and a durable cover. The foam is designed to absorb impact, reduce vibration transfer, return to shape and minimise rebound for safer dropping.

## Brief Description

- Size per pad: 100 × 60 × 15 cm
- Sold as a pair
- Weight: 15 kg each; 30 kg per pair
- Fire-retardant casing and high-impact construction
- Intended for home gyms, commercial gyms, weightlifting clubs and CrossFit classes
