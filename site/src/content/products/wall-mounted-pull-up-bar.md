## Feature

Wall Mounted Pull-Up Bar is suitable for gym, commercial and home use. It can be used alongside resistance bands, TRX systems, Olympic bars, push-up accessories and benches.

## Brief Description

- Overall size: 1600 × 1100 × 2500 mm
- 2 × 2510 mm uprights
- 2 × 1070 mm single crossbeams
- 1 × 1570 mm double crossbeam
- 2 × J-hooks
- All nuts and bolts included
