## Feature

Wooden Jerk Boxes are designed for jerk training, partial clean and jerk work, deadlift training and plyometrics. The system combines stackable, interlocking risers and platforms with internal bracing. Top stoppers help prevent a barbell from rolling off the stack.

## Brief Description

- 8 blocks per set: 2 × 2.25 in, 2 × 6 in, 2 × 12 in and 2 × 15 in
- Block footprint: 91 × 51 cm
- Adjustable height: approximately 6–89 cm
- Complete-set weight: 172 kg
- Fully assembled; the 2.25 in block must be on top when in use
