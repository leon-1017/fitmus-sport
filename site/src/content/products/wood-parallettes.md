## Feature

Wood Parallettes use 1.5 in (38 mm) wooden rails with half-dome end caps. They provide a compact alternative to full-size parallel bars for bodyweight training.

They can be used for push-ups and dips, as well as static holds such as L-sits and planches. The compact format also supports progressive handstand and gymnastic-style training.

## Brief Description

- Approximate size: 9.68 in high × 17.5 in long
- Rail diameter: 1.5 in hardwood dowel
- Suitable for CrossFit, HIIT-style exercise, commercial fitness centres and home gyms
