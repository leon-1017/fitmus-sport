Training is only one part of a sustainable fitness routine. The original article lists foods that can fit an active diet:

- Lentils
- Banana and whey-protein smoothie
- Coconut oil
- Nuts and seeds
- Sweet potatoes
- Banana with peanut butter
- Eggs
- Brown rice
- Almond butter
- Oatmeal
- Low-fat chocolate milk

Individual needs differ by training load, health, allergies and goals. Use the list as ideas rather than a prescribed diet, and seek qualified nutrition advice when needed.
