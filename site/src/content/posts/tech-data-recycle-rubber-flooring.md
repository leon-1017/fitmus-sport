<p>Recycle Rubber flooring is suitable for all applications which require attenuation of impact sound and vibration, durability and resistance to mechanical loads. It can be used for a wide variety of sports surfaces, floor covering, impact protection, sheets and tiles for building protection and for impact sound protection, materials for vibration technology and anti-slip mats for securing loads. It can be used for a large variety of applications, it is long-lasting, resilient and can be recycled.<br>
In addition to Pur-bonding agents, we made primarily of the following elastomers (depending on material type)<br>
SBR/NBR rubber granules and fibres<br>
EPDM granules<br>
Other elastomers and additives</p>
