Recycled rubber flooring is suitable wherever impact sound, vibration, durability and resistance to mechanical loads matter. It can be used for sports surfaces, floor coverings, impact protection, sheets and tiles, vibration technology and anti-slip load mats.

## Material and application areas

Depending on the material type, the flooring can use SBR or NBR rubber granules and fibres, EPDM granules and other elastomers or additives. Applications include:

- Indoor and outdoor floor coverings
- Impact-sound insulating layers
- Impact-protection flooring and elastic components
- Vibration and shock absorption
- Building-protection mats and vegetation-control mats
- Protection against point and surface loads

The material is resilient, long-lasting and recyclable.
