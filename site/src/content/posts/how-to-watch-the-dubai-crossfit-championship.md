<p>The 2019 CrossFit Games season officially kicks off in less than two weeks.The Dubai CrossFit Championship will start things off Wednesday, December 12th, as it breaks new ground as the first sanctioned event to take place under the new season format.Only one man, one woman, and one team in this ultra competitive field will be crowned champion and receive the opening invites to the 2019 Reebok CrossFit Games.</p>
<p>Since most of you probably won’t be catching the event in person, here’s a rundown on how you can watch the event from the comfort of your own home and stay up to date on all news coming out of the City of Gold.</p>
<p><strong>Livestream</strong><br>
Every heat of every event will be live streamed on multiple platforms for you to choose from:<br>
Event Website: https://dubaicrossfitchampionship.com/<br>
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/user/dxbfitnesschamp/<br>
All events will immediately be archived on YouTube afterwards so you can watch them at your convenience.</p>
<h4>Event Times</h4>
<p>Events will take place during the following times.<br>
Wednesday, December 12: 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM&nbsp;GMT+4 (Tuesday, 8:00 PM PT – Wednesday, 1:00 AM PT)<br>
Thursday, December 13:&nbsp;8:00 – 11:00 AM&nbsp;GMT+4&nbsp;(Wednesday, 8:00 PM PT – 11:00 PM PT)<br>
Friday, December 14: 1:00 PM – 8:00 PM&nbsp;GMT+4 (Friday, 1:00 AM PT – 8:00 AM PT)<br>
Saturday, December 15: 1:00 PM – 8:00 PM&nbsp;GMT+4&nbsp;(Saturday, 1:00 AM PT – 8:00 AM PT)</p>
<p>Social Media<br>
If you just can’t bear to be off “the ‘gram” or Mark Zuckerberg is your idol then you can get curated news and updates from the event via their social media pages.</p>
<p>Instagram: @dxbfitnesschamp<br>
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/DubaiCrossFitChampionship/<br>
CrossFit Games Instagram: @crossfitgames</p>
