The Synrgy 360 system responds to the need for flexible functional-fitness space. Its freestanding frame can be reconfigured with suspension bands, battle ropes, heavy bags, dumbbells, resistance bands and squat-rack accessories.

The frame itself supports bodyweight work such as pull-ups and assisted push-ups. Multiple stations let an individual or a group rotate through a circuit, while attachment points and integrated storage help facilities create a layout that matches their programmes.
