## What is IWF?

The International Weightlifting Federation (IWF) is the international governing body for weightlifting, recognised by the International Olympic Committee. It promotes and governs the sport, supports fair-play competition and provides equal conditions for athletes across its member federations.

## IWF bar specifications

Only barbells meeting IWF specifications and approval may be used in weightlifting competitions under its jurisdiction.

### Men's bar

- Weight: 20 kg
- Length: 220 cm
- Chromed steel with knurled grip section
- Sleeve diameter: 5 cm; sleeve length: 41.5 cm
- Grip diameter: 2.8 cm; grip section: 131 cm
- Centre knurling: 12 cm
- Blue identification markings
- Tolerance: +0.1% to -0.05%

### Women's bar

- Weight: 15 kg
- Length: 201 cm
- Chromed steel with knurled grip section
- Sleeve diameter: 5 cm; sleeve length: 32 cm
- Grip diameter: 2.5 cm; grip section: 131 cm
- Yellow identification markings
- Tolerance: +0.1% to -0.05%

IWF competition equipment also includes colour-coded discs, two 2.5 kg collars per barbell and compliant electronic scales.
