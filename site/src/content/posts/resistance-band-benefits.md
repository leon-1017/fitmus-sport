Resistance bands are lightweight, versatile tools that can be used on their own or looped around a rack or stand. They can also be anchored with the feet or shortened with a knot for lower-body work.

## What resistance bands do

Bands can help to build strength, improve mobility, support rehabilitation, make weight training more challenging and assist with pull-ups. Flat bands are commonly available in progressive levels: thinner bands stretch more easily, while thicker bands provide greater resistance and counterbalance.

## Ten useful benefits

1. Improve exercise quality.
2. Encourage controlled movement.
3. Recruit stabilising muscles.
4. Support functional training.
5. Provide an alternative to machines.
6. Travel easily because they are lightweight.
7. Work well with compound exercises.
8. Let you change the angle of resistance.
9. Encourage better form.
10. Help you feel which muscles are working.
