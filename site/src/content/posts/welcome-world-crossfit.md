CrossFit combines constantly varied functional movement with high-intensity work, measurable progress and a strong training community. Its workouts draw from gymnastics, Olympic weightlifting, running and rowing and develop endurance, stamina, strength, flexibility, power, speed, coordination, agility, balance and accuracy.

## What to expect

Daily WODs vary by box and coach. Sessions may be short but demanding, and movements can be scaled for beginners or anyone returning from a limitation. A good onboarding process teaches technique before full-intensity workouts, while coaches help members recognise fatigue, pain and overtraining.

Time, repetitions and loads provide useful benchmarks. Record them to measure personal progress rather than treating the whiteboard as a reason to ignore form or recovery.
