<p>The 34th China International Sporting Goods Show 2016</p>
<p>As the largest and most influential sporting goods exhibition in the Asia-Pacific region and the only national-class, internationalized and professionalized trade show for the sports industry in China, the 34th China International Sporting Goods Show 2016 (China Sport Show 2016) is to be staged from 22nd – 25th April 2015 at Fuzhou Strait International Conference &amp; Exhibition Center (SICEC).</p>
<p>With an exhibition area of 120,000 ㎡ and divided into six themed exhibition zones, The 34th China International Sporting Goods Show 2016 (China Sport Show 2016) will attract more than 1000 famous enterprises home and aboard to attend.</p>
<p>Summit and forums will be held for visitors &amp; professionals conjunction to China Sport Show 2016 to make an omni-directional flagship sporting goods event.</p>
