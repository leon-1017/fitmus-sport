<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What is a foam roller?</strong></span><br>
Foam rollers are exercise devices used for massage and fitness. While usually long and cylindrical, they come in many shapes, sizes and varying textures. When used for self-massage, they help soothe tight, sore areas and speed up muscle recovery. This process of rolling out tight muscles and relieving tension is also called myofascial release.</p>
<p><strong>Why You Need a Foam Roller?</strong></p>
<p>Using a foam roller is the best way to perform self-massage and myofascial&nbsp;release.For anyone who exercises regularly, or has muscle tightness, imbalances or a history of injury, using a foam roller on a regular basis can help loosen tight muscles, reduce muscle adhesions&nbsp;and scar tissue formation, increase flexibility.</p>
