<h2>CrossFit gyms: Key differences</h2>
<p>Opening a CrossFit gym is a lot like opening a regular gym, but there are a lot of key differences that set it apart, making the opening process more challenging in some ways. These six steps to opening are straight from CrossFit’s website:</p>
<p>1.Become a CrossFit affiliate: To open a CrossFit gym and be able to use the actual name, you must be an affiliate, a process that requires an application and an essay that includes your background and reasons why CrossFit is important to you.</p>
<p>CrossFit affiliation is an Internet-based, grassroots movement started by CrossFitters who wanted their own local CrossFit-equipped gyms, trainers, and communities. ..Write an essay <a href="https://www.crossfit.com/affiliate/how-to" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">(application)</a> to crossfit Inc, license a name, set up a website, send&nbsp; photos and you become part of the growing community of CrossFit affiliates.</p>
<p>2.The Cost of becoming a Crossfit Affiliate:<br>
A yearly fee of USD$3000 for every licensed CrossFit Affiliate box, plus a mandatory USD$1000 CrossFit Level-1 certificate for each trainer coaching in a box, adds up to a hefty ammount.</p>
<p>3.After registering as an affiliate, you must have a website for CrossFit to list your box on their website.This makes it easy for people to search for the nearest box in their area, so you’ll want to be on the site.</p>
<p>4-you might want to think about the type of equipment you have in your gym. you’ll want to consider using that equipment in your new box. <a href="https://fitmus-sport.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Contact us</a> for getting your crossfit box equipment.</p>
<p>5-Space: Time to get creative. if You’re not limited with space, so feel free to get creative.</p>
<p>6-Marketing: As you’re probably already familiar, membership prices are high, and that means you have to work hard to convince the community that it’s worth the price.</p>
<p>7-As a gym owner, you’re going to be in higher management position, but as a CrossFit gym owner, this is going to look a little different for you. You’ll be the most knowledgeable about CrossFit in your gym, so you’ll want to be comfortable coaching and interacting with your members. You’re cultivating a community of motivation–be a part of it.</p>
