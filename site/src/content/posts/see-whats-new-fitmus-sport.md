Fitmus Sport develops new kettlebell ideas for OEM customers. The 3D-scanned designs combine a smooth, ergonomic handle with a flat base that prevents rolling and supports balance.

## Design details

- High-quality iron casting with a refined handle finish
- 10 kg to 32 kg size range
- Flat base and balanced construction
- Engraved black lettering or laser-etched custom imagery
- Custom colours and lettering available

Kettlebells can support total-body strength, power, endurance, dynamic flexibility, core stability, agility and coordination. Typical movements include squats, throws, cleans, jerks, snatches and rotational swings.
