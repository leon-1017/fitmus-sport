<p>How to Open a Crossfit Gym In India?<br>
1.Sign up for a workshop to become certified as a Level 1 trainer (costs $1,000)<br>
2.Pay the annual licensing fee to CrossFit (costs $3,000)<br>
3.Find a convenient location to open your gym<br>
4.Determine what to charge members (standard membership is $100 to $200 per month)<br>
5.Set up specialty classes to set you apart from the competition<br>
6.When you’re ready to hire, make sure you find good employees and coaches<br>
7.Use social media to involve members and market your gym<br>
8.Keep track of all of your expenses<br>
9.Grow your network with other box owners</p>
