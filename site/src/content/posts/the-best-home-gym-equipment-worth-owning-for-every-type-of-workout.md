You do not need a large commercial gym to train at home. A practical setup can combine these compact, versatile tools:

- TRX suspension training kit
- Cast-iron kettlebell
- Textured medicine ball
- Adjustable jump rope
- Protective floor tiles
- Foam roller
- Pull-up bar
- Stable ab wheel
- Resistance band
- Step platform
- Adjustable bench
- Exercise mat

Choose equipment that matches the workouts you actually enjoy and that can be stored safely in the space available.
