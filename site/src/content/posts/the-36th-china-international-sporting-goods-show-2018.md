The 36th China International Sporting Goods Show was held from May 25–28, 2018 at the National Exhibition and Convention Center in Shanghai.

China Sport Show is a national-level, international and professional sporting-goods exhibition for the Asia-Pacific region. The 2018 event covered fitness equipment, stadium facilities and flooring, sportswear, outdoor and leisure equipment, ball games, racket sports and combat-sports facilities.

The show also offered fashion events, seminars and keynote sessions. Manufacturers, distributors, retailers, importers, exporters, sports associations and facility owners attended to explore products, technology, marketing resources and new sports-industry ideas.
