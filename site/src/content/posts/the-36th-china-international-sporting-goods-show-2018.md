<p>China Sport Show is the largest and most influential sporting goods exhibition in the Asia-Pacific region and the only national-class, internationalized and professionalized trade show for the sports industry in China. Its aim is to show the top quality of Chinese sporting goods and to boost chinese sports brands in the world. Besides the large exhibition space China Sport Show offers daily fashion shows and free seminar series and keynote speakers.</p>
<p>China Sport Show has been held successfully for 35 years, and attracted 1473 exhibitors and over 91,000 visiters last years. In 2018, the Show will feature fitness equipment, stadium facilities, construction and flooring, sportswear, outdoor and leisure facilities, ball games, racket games and combat sports facilities.</p>
<p>The 36th China International Sporting Goods Show 2018 have been staged on May 25-28, 2018 at National Exhibition and Convention Center (Shanghai) (NECC), China.&nbsp;The only national-level international and professional sporting goods exhibition in China.The largest and most authoritative sporting goods exhibition in Asian-Pacific. A shortcut for international sporting brands to enter China, an important window for Chinese sporting brands to demonstrate their strength to the world. A platform of comprehensive sports industry embracing sporting goods, sports marketing resources, sports culture and technology. A booster for sporting goods enterprises to promote brands and to expand marketing channels. An important channel for diffusing the latest concepts of sports culture. Exhibition area will reach 200,000 square meters. More than 1100 exhibitors home and abroad and 100000 visitors will participate in the exhibition.</p>
<p>Exhibitors showcase their products on manufacturers and dealers of fitness equipment, footwear, sportswear, ball games, tennis, badminton, cycling, stadium faclities, stadium construction, roller-skating and running equipment.</p>
