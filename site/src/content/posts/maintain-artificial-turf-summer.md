<p>In summer, the temperature is relatively high and the artificial turf material is flammable. How do we maintain artificial turf in the summer?Next, small detail for everyone:</p>
<p>1. When the hot summer temperature suddenly increases, the temperature of the artificial turf may increase. At this time, we can cool the lawn with water to make the athlete cool and comfortable. This method is also a kind of clean for the lawn itself, of course, the number of watering should not be too frequent.after the big event, grass needs to perform some cleanup, and manual cleaning</p>
<p>2.Control the use of artificial turf, especially when using high frequency of summer ban lawn threw cigarette butts and corrosive liquids such as soda and so on</p>
<p>3.Clean up trash on the lawn in a timely manner, especially hard objects, otherwise it hurts</p>
<p>4.Check and repair maintenance of artificial turf. Check whether there is any looseness in the suture at any time during the use of artificial turf. Whether the bottom of the turf is damaged, torn, or burned. If there is a large repair, you should contact the pavement company in a timely manner. Do not save yourself money for fear of trouble, so as to avoid unnecessary losses.</p>
