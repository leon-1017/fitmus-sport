Skullbells bring a distinctive look to strength and conditioning training while retaining the function of a traditional kettlebell. The one-piece cast design combines a specialised grip texture with a durable coating intended to withstand repeated training.

## Features

- Skull-shaped design with a range of sizes
- Handle sized for one- or two-hand swings
- Flat base for renegade rows and handstands
- Ergonomic shape to support forearm comfort during overhead movements
- Suitable for CrossFit, HIIT, strength, toning and active recovery

The compact bell can be used by beginners and experienced athletes for swings, cleans, presses and other full-body movements. Use controlled technique, especially during snatches and overhead work.
