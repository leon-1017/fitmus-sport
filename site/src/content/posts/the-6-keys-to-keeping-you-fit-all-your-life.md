Fitness is a long-term journey rather than a short project. The original article recommends six habits for staying active over the years:

1. Stop obsessing over appearance and focus on health and quality of life.
2. Train for performance, not only to look better.
3. Do some form of activity every day, even when a full gym session is not possible.
4. Train with a goal and replace it with a new one when it is achieved.
5. Keep variety in your programme so different muscles and skills are developed.
6. Allow recovery and rest so training remains sustainable.

Regular, enjoyable activity is easier to maintain than a routine built around punishment or unrealistic promises.
