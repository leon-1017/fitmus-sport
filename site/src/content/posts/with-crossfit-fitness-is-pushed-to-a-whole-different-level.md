CrossFit workouts combine gymnastics, weightlifting, running, rowing and endurance movements in timed or set-based workouts of the day. Group sessions commonly include a warm-up, coached work and cool-down.

The programme created a distinct fitness community in Malaysia after its introduction there. Participants describe benefits in stamina, strength, movement understanding and social connection. The article also notes the importance of learning why a movement works and which muscles it uses, not simply chasing intensity.
