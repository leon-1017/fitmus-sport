A stability ball adds an unstable surface that challenges balance, coordination and the smaller muscles that support larger movements. The original selection includes:

1. Wall squat
2. Single-leg wall squat
3. Balancing reverse lunge
4. Balancing push-up
5. Plank to pike
6. Ball pass
7. Balancing oblique crunch
8. Glute bridge to hamstring curl
9. Lower-back extension

Use a clear area, choose the right ball size and progress from stable variations before adding speed or range.
