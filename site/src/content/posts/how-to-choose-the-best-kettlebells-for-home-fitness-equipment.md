<p>1- SMOOTH FINISH, Pay close attention to the finish when comparing to images of others supplier. Our Powder Coat Kettlebells have a smoother finish because our casting process creates a smoother surface to powder coat over. Having a smooth finish protects your hands, wrists and forearms</p>
<p>2- Handle Seam (Flashing),The process of casting a kettlebell leaves a seam (or flashing) across the middle of the underside of the handle. Check our kettlebell Handles,This seam has been filed down before the kettlebell is coated or finished so that there is a nice, even surface to hold on to. Cheaper kettlebell manufacturers will make no real effort to remove this sharp seam, and your hands will suffer for it (and potentially bleed from it).</p>
<p>3- Handle Diameter. our kettlebell handles will get thicker as the weight goes up.our handle diameters starting at about 30-31 mm and go up to around 38 mm for the heaviest bells.</p>
<p>4-Handle Inner Width, you need both hands to be able to fit within the handle without it being too tight or uncomfortable.
