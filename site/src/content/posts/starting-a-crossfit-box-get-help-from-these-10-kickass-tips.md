Opening a CrossFit box combines business planning with a commitment to the training community. The original Fitmus article highlights ten practical considerations:

1. Complete a Level 1 trainer workshop.
2. Pay the annual CrossFit licensing fee.
3. Find a convenient location.
4. Set a membership price that fits the market.
5. Offer specialty classes to differentiate the box.
6. Hire capable employees and coaches.
7. Use social media to involve members and market the gym.
8. Track every expense.
9. Build a network with other box owners.
10. Expect challenges and plan for them.

A successful box is more than a room for workouts: it is a community and a lifestyle that members want to share.
