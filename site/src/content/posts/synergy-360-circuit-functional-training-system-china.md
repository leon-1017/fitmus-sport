Modular functional-training systems help facility owners create dedicated training space without committing to a single fixed routine. Open frames can be configured around suspension trainers, battle ropes, heavy bags, dumbbells, resistance bands and squat stations.

The SYNRGY 360 concept is designed as a training centre for individual or group circuits. Members can use the frame for pull-ups and assisted push-ups, while multiple attachment points and integrated storage support different programmes and facility layouts.

Its modular design can be tailored to training objectives, making it suitable for CrossFit, TRX, high-intensity interval training and broader functional-fitness programming.
