Small, portable training tools can make home workouts easier to start and easier to repeat. The original Fitmus article highlights six compact accessories:

1. **Foam roller** — helps release muscle tension, support circulation and add instability or self-massage work.
2. **Balance board** — trains balance and deep stabilising muscles with low joint impact.
3. **Jump rope** — raises heart rate, improves conditioning and trains the legs, abdomen and shoulders.
4. **Abdominal turbine** — challenges trunk stability as the wheel rolls forward and returns under control.
5. **Gymnastic rings** — add bodyweight pulling and support work when securely installed.

These tools take up much less space than large cardio machines and can be stored or transported easily.
