CrossFit combines constantly varied, high-intensity functional movement with coaching and community. Five principles can make the first months more approachable:

1. **Forget the stereotypes.** Good coaches prioritise safe form and stop athletes from doing more than they can control.
2. **Do not wait to get in shape.** Classes can be scaled for beginners; you do not need to be fit before joining.
3. **Pick the right box.** Visit local gyms, ask questions and choose one whose focus and coaching match your goals.
4. **Bring the right mindset.** Leave ego at the door; CrossFit communities include many ages, backgrounds and ability levels.
5. **Do not quit too soon.** Give the process time to develop, especially through the first 90 days.
