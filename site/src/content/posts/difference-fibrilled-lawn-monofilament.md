<p>The vast majority of the padel courts we play on are artificial turf, but did you know that there are different types of turf? We explain in this post the difference between fibrillated and monofilament grass.</p>
<p>Fibrillated grass: it is a fiber with a flat strip that receives small longitudinal cuts in its manufacture.</p>

<p>High UV Resistance :<br>
The unique element and technology ensure the perfect performance under high UV area .</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Monofilament grass:it is a thread consisting of a series of continuous filaments. They are created with independent stitches, therefore, they do not fray or change shape. It is newer and does not give compaction problems such as fibrillated grass.</p>
